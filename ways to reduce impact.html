Ways To Reduce Impact
How can we minimize the impact of computer device production on the environment?
Choosing energy-efficient devices Turning off devices when not being used Upgrading certain devices Update devices less often
  • When it comes to purchasing a new computer, look for Energy Star labels as they use less electricity and deliver the same performance compared to other computers
  • Simply powering down or plugging your computer, monitor or other parts can completely decrease the amount of energy used when powered on.
  • Dont put your devices on sleep mode as they still use energy
  • Instead of replacing your whole computer, upgrading the components inside the computer(such as RAM, memory, etc...) is a better option as they matter most of the computer compared to the case and fans.
  • Removing dust from your computer twice every year can surprisingly expand your devices lifespan
  • As you update one of your drivers from your computer, it eventually becomes slower and slower with every update which in the end, tells you it's slow and you need to buy a new device for faster performance.
