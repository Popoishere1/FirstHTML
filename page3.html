Minimizing Impact
How can we minimize the impact of computer device production on the environment?
Changing into renewable energy sources Using more efficient materials
  • Manufacturers who make our products should switch to renewable energy as energy consumption decreases during production and at the same time, can reduce their carbon footprints
  • Manufacturers can lower the use of raw materials(gold, tin, cadmium, etc...) by adjusting the amount needed for their devices.
  • A good start is to make more efficient devices that require less materials without its performance decreasing.
