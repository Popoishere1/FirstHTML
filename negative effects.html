What are the negative effects of computer device disposal?
Greenhouse gases being released Water being polluted
Some areas dispose e-waste by burning(incineration) it which is extremely dangerous as it releases toxic chemicals into the air. If anyone is exposed to these chemicals, it exposes them to respiratory problems, certain skin diseases, and some forms of long-term cancers. E-waste being disposed improperly by incineration, the toxic chemicals are being released into the water and those whose water sources get contaminated such as humans, animals, and plants are effected.
