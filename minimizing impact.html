How can we minimize the impact of computer device production on the environment?

Changing into renewable energy sources

The manufacturers who make our products should switch to renewable energy as energy consumption decreases during production and at the same time, can reduce their carbon footprints

Using more efficient materials

Manufacturers can lower the use of raw materials(gold, tin, cadmium, etc...) by adjusting the amount needed for their devices. A good start is to make more efficient devices that require less materials without its performance decreasing
